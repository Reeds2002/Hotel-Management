Welcome to Skye Resorts and Hotels, a luxurious resort and hotel getaway that promises to take your breath away.


Since its establishment in 1997, by the elustrius Mr.Rhesing, Skye Resorts and Hotels has become a go-to destination for those seeking a unique, luxurious experience. Our resorts offer the highest quality amenities, from spas with relaxing treatments to spacious, well-appointed rooms with all the comforts of home. We believe in taking the time to make sure each guest truly enjoys their stay with us, and our staff is always available to help you create the perfect getaway. At Skye Resorts and Hotels, we strive to provide a luxurious experience to each of our guests. We understand that everyone’s idea of luxury is different, so we’ve made sure to offer a variety of amenities and services to meet any need. Whether you’re looking for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a corporate retreat, we have something to suit you.


Our resorts are located in some of the most beautiful locations around the world, from the sunny beaches of California to the stunning mountains of New Zealand. No matter where you stay, you can be sure of an unforgettable experience. With our commitment to providing quality service and great value, you’ll find yourself wanting to come back time and time again. At Skye Resorts and Hotels, we understand that life is all about balance. That’s why we offer a range of activities to suit any taste. From swimming and tennis to wine tasting and spa treatments, you’ll find something to help you relax and enjoy your stay.


We also believe in being good stewards of the environment. We strive to make sure our resorts are eco-friendly, with sustainable practices and a commitment to minimizing our impact on the environment. At Skye Resorts and Hotels, we strive to make sure our guests have a truly unique and luxurious experience. With our commitment to quality service, great value, and eco-friendly practices, you can be sure that you’ll have a stay that you’ll never forget. So come stay with us and let us show you why Skye Resorts and Hotels is the perfect destination for your next getaway.
