Frequently Asked Questions

Q1. What are the check-in and check-out timings?

Our check-in time is after 3:00 PM and check-out time is before 11:00 AM. Please note that early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.



Q2. What type of amenities are available?

Our amenities include a heated outdoor pool, fitness center, and complimentary high-speed WiFi in all our rooms and public areas. A wide range of additional services such as laundry, dry cleaning, and concierge are also available.



Q3. Are there any pet-friendly rooms?

Yes, we offer pet-friendly rooms upon request. A $50 pet fee per night is applicable.



Q4. Is there a restaurant onsite?

Yes, we have an onsite restaurant, The Bistro, serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner. We also offer room service for guests who prefer to enjoy their meal in the comfort of their rooms.



Q5. Is parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary self-parking for our guests.



Q6. Are there any safety features in the rooms?

Yes, all our rooms are equipped with smoke detectors, fire alarms, and safety locks. The entire premise is CCTV monitored.
